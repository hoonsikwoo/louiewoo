Fashion Show

What is a fashion show?

It is simpler than you think.

  • It's a show presenting a selection of new clothes ans styles of the upcoming season by designers and brands of different cities.
  • It gives people an insight on what to wear tomorrow or even in different occasions.
  • The show's goal is to give courage to people who want to try new things.
  • Viewers gain a wider perspective in styles and a wider spectrum of outfits that they put on.
Visit Site →

Why it isn't intimidating

There are many misconceptions on fashion shows even before people start watching. But they are not true!

  • Fashion show introduces styles that are totally different from the past, and sometimes fight the fixed sterotype.
  • They tend to be very challenging and experimental, but even the designers rarely sell clothes directly from the runway.
  • We don't have to be experts to watch the show. It is to give ideas on the new trend for large public.
  • We don't need to study the history of fashion. Just looking at the big picture like an artwork will help.
